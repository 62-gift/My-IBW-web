Who We Are

IWB (Innovative Waste Busters) is a Lesotho-based company dedicated to electronic waste recycling and digital security solutions. Founded in 2024 with a capital of M100,000, IWB was established under the leadership of Co-Founder Kenneth. As the company grew, it attracted new partnerships, including Shadrack, who joined as the second CEO.

Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize electronic recycling by reducing e-waste and promoting sustainable technology use. Our services include recycling RAM, hard drives, and motherboard components while also providing secure data recovery and destruction solutions.
